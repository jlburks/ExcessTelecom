Internet Service and Low-Cost Devices through the Affordable Connectivity Program (ACP), Lifeline and New Connection Plans

New enrollments into the Affordable Connectivity Program (ACP) stopped on February 7, 2024 and the ACP will run through April unless the Federal government approves more funding. Customers enrolled in the ACP as of February 8, 2024 will continue to receive ACP coverage unless the ACP ends or they lose program eligibility. At Excess Telecom, we're dedicated to ensuring that you and your family continue to enjoy reliable internet access through the ACP and beyond.
